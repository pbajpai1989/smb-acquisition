What to watch at month 2

New beats old journey across both segments

Positive avg and a positive overall median vs old journey is the clearest win so far. The new journey is already doing better than what it replaced.

KM vs holdout shows real broad lift

In Key Markets, the new journey beats no-journey with a median > avg — the strongest quality signal in the data. Most creators there are genuinely benefiting.

KM vs old journey: will the median move?

$0.0 median with $7.9 avg means a small cohort is driving KM gains over the old journey. Watch whether this broadens to the typical creator by month 2.

Overall vs holdout: too early to call

Slightly negative median (-$0.2) outside KM is a flag but not a verdict at one month. Could be cohort timing or noise. Needs another data pull to assess.
